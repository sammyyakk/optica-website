We are proud to announce **AI Conclave 2026**, BVP Optica’s **first-ever national-level AI hackathon** designed to empower students to build impactful, ethical, and scalable Artificial Intelligence solutions for real-world challenges.

Hosted at **Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, West Delhi**, AI Conclave brings together innovators from diverse academic backgrounds to collaborate, ideate, and create technology that matters.

<Callout type="info" title="What Makes AI Conclave Special">
AI Conclave focuses not only on building models, but also on **problem understanding, explainability, responsible AI practices, and real-world deployment readiness**.
</Callout>

---

## Event Overview

AI Conclave is a **multidisciplinary technical hackathon** centered around applying Artificial Intelligence across high-impact domains such as:

- Environment & Sustainability  
- Healthcare  
- FinTech  
- Smart Infrastructure  
- Education  
- Social Good  

Participants will progress through multiple evaluation stages, receiving feedback and mentorship while refining their solutions.

---

## Host Institution

**Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering**  
West Delhi, Delhi, India

---

## Who Can Participate?

The hackathon is open to students from all academic backgrounds:

- Undergraduate students  
- Postgraduate students  
- Engineering  
- Management  
- Arts, Commerce & Sciences  
- Law  
- Medical  
- Other disciplines  

No prior hackathon experience required — just curiosity and a willingness to learn.

---

## Team Composition

- Team Size: **1 – 4 Members**

---

## Focus Areas

Participants may work in (but are not limited to):

- Artificial Intelligence  
- Machine Learning  
- Data Science  
- Data Analytics  
- Product-Focused Solutions

## Stages & Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Submission Round (Online)">
    **01 Feb 2026, 12:00 PM IST → 14 Feb 2026, 12:00 PM IST**  
    Teams submit their ideas and problem statements via the **Unstop platform**.  
    This is the first filtering round.
  </Step>

  <Step number={2} title="Online Presentation Round">
    **16 Feb 2026, 12:00 PM IST → 08:00 PM IST**  
    Selected teams present their ideas on **Discord** using their preferred medium.  
    Judging based on clarity, feasibility, innovation, and relevance.
  </Step>

  <Step number={3} title="Offline / On-Campus Round">
    **18 Feb 2026, 07:00 AM IST → 05:00 PM IST**  
    Finalists compete on campus with live evaluation and mentoring.
  </Step>
</Steps>
