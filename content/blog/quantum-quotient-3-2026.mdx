## Competition Format

### The Challenge

**30 Questions | 30 Minutes | 100% Focus**

Participants will answer 30 carefully curated multiple-choice questions covering:

- **Light Fundamentals**: Reflection, refraction, and basic wave properties  
- **Wave Optics**: Interference, diffraction, and polarization  
- **Modern Optics**: Fiber optics, laser physics, and photonic devices  
- **Advanced Concepts**: Quantum optics, nonlinear optics, and optical instruments

### No Safety Nets

- ❌ No calculators allowed  
- ❌ No external resources  
- ❌ No internet searches  
- ✅ Just your knowledge, intuition, and speed!

## How to Prepare

### Study Topics
Focus on core concepts in optics and photonics:
- Ray optics (mirrors, lenses, prisms)
- Wave nature of light (interference, diffraction)
- Electromagnetic theory of light
- Optical instruments and devices
- Laser physics and fiber optics
- Modern quantum optics

### Practice
- Solve previous year questions if available
- Join the Warm-Up Round on Instagram for a sneak peek
- Revise formulas and key principles
- Practice quick mental calculations

### Mindset
- Stay calm under time pressure
- Read questions carefully
- Don't overthink—trust your preparation
