We are thrilled to announce the **AI Solution Expo 2026**, BVP Optica’s upcoming flagship exhibition designed to highlight the next generation of AI-driven solutions and innovations. 

Hosted at **Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, New Delhi**, the Expo offers an incredible platform for students to present their innovative AI models, research projects, and deep learning solutions designed to tackle real-world problems.

<Callout type="info" title="What to Expect">
The AI Solution Expo goes beyond traditional presentations. It's an interactive environment aimed at **networking with industry experts, attending hands-on workshops, and demonstrating real, deployable AI projects**.
</Callout>

---

## Event Overview

The AI Solution Expo is an interactive exhibition where participants showcase Artificial Intelligence applications across various high-impact domains. Attendees and participants will have the chance to:

- View live project showcases from top teams
- Experience interactive AI demonstrations
- Network with industry leaders and peers
- Attend workshops on generative AI and ML
- Compete for prizes and certificates for top solutions

## Who Can Participate?

The Expo is open to students from all academic backgrounds who are passionate about AI:

- Undergraduate students  
- Postgraduate students  
- Anyone with a functioning AI model, prototype, or research presentation 

No prior exhibition experience is required — just curiosity, a working project, and a willingness to share your innovation!

---

## Why Attend or Participate?

- **Real-World Impact:** Build and present scalable AI solutions to tangible problems
- **Networking:** Connect with peers, mentors, and industry professionals
- **Skill Enhancement:** Gain experience in communicating complex technical ideas visually and verbally
- **Recognition:** Earning certificates and competing for exciting prizes

<Callout type="success" title="Outcome-Oriented Learning">
Participants will walk away with practical experience in evaluating AI responsibly — from understanding data constraints to pitching deployment-ready architectures to experts.
</Callout>
